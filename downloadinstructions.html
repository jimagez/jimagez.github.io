Image Download Instructions

Computer Instructions:

1) Right-click the image you want to download.

2) Click 'Save image as...'

3) Select a place and/or name to save the image and

You're done!

Mobile Device Instructions:

1) Press and hold on the image you want to download.

2) When prompted, press 'Save Image' and

You're done!

The image will be in your photo library.
